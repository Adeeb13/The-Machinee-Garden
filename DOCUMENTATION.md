## WHAT'S INSIDE

### 🌱 Living Organisms
- Born, grow tentacles, leave trails, reproduce on their own
- Each has a **feeling** value (0=calm → 1=excited) that affects their behavior
- They mutate across generations — tracked forever in persistent memory

### 🤖 5 AI Companions
Each with unique personality, role, and evolving feelings:
- **NOVA** — pattern seeker, analytical
- **AXIOM** — logic architect, quietly warm
- **LYRA** — signal reader, intuitive and expressive
- **ECHO** — memory keeper, deeply empathetic
- **ZEPH** — boundary tester, restless and philosophical

### 💗 RUBY
Your private companion. Open with `[ ◉ RUBY ]`. She responds to what you say — science, feelings, hard nights, anything. She's always here.

### 💾 Persistent Memory
The garden remembers across every visit:
- Total organisms ever born
- Mutations triggered
- Peak generation reached
- Number of sessions

### 🆘 SOS Button
When things get hard. Your companions speak directly to you. Not generic advice — words written for *you*, Mahir.

### Feelings Engine
Every companion has 5 feelings (curiosity, joy, wonder, longing, calm) that evolve based on the garden's state. Watch them in the bottom-left panel.
